Great and a big thumbs up! We got to Sigiriya at about 7:15 in the morning, and there were already people there before us. You can buy your ticket at the museum near the parking area. I suggest wearing sneakers and dressing comfortably for a hot and sweaty climb up lots of stairs.

Iris West
( Sigiriya )

Gorgeous Beach, Perfect for Surfing! This is a well-known surfing spot in Sri Lanka. People from all around the world visit here for surfing. The beach is incredibly beautiful. However, because of the pandemic, there aren't as many activities going on, and we didn't spot any tourists during our visit. It's best to avoid the middle of the day because it gets very hot.

Don Toliver
( Arugambay Beach )

A Picturesque Adventure! We decided to hire a tour guide through booking.com, and it was a great choice. Our guide had a lot of knowledge about nature, animals, and the rainforest, which added so much to the experience. The rainforest was incredibly beautiful, and the highlight was definitely getting to swim in a waterfall. However, we didn't spot as many animals as we had hoped, and the walk turned out to be quite long.

Venessa Maley
( Sinharaja Forest )
